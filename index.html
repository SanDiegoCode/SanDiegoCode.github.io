What we do

At San Diego Code, we believe that everyone, from elementary school students to senior citizens, have the capability to code. That's why we dedicate our time to developing programs to teach computer science to all.

Summer Coding Camp

Interactive, engaging camps offered during the summer.

During the summer, we offer free coding camps targeted towards middle and high school students. At these camps, we teach the fundementals of Java, a useful langauge, especially for those planning on taking the AP Computer Science class.

We are currently working on trying to offer classes towards elementary school students as well as senior citizens.

Monthly Coding Workshops

Open 2 hour sessions where you can work at your own pace.

At each of these sessions, we'll guide you through labs designed to be done at your own pace. Our instructors will help you whenever needed, and you don't need to worry about being behind - everyone will be at different levels.
